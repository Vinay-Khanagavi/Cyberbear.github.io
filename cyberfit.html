What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is like having a special shield to protect your online adventures! 🌟 It helps keep your personal information safe from sneaky tricks and bad guys on the internet, so you can explore and have fun without worries. It stops bad people from stealing our stuff and keeps our online world safe, just like locking our doors keeps our homes safe! 🏠🔒

Why is Cyber Security Super Important?

🛡️ Protects Your Secrets: Keeps your photos, messages, and personal info safe from strangers!
🎮 Safe Gaming: Lets you play online games without getting tricked by fake friends!
💳 Money Safety: Protects your family's money and important information!
🌐 Happy Internet: Makes sure everyone can have fun online safely!

Cyber Security Super Powers!

🎣
Phishing Protection
Phishing is like fake fishing! Bad guys send fake emails or messages pretending to be someone you trust. Always ask a grown-up before clicking links from strangers!
🦠
Malware Shield
Malware are like digital germs that can make computers sick! They hide in downloads and websites. Only download from safe places that grown-ups approve!
🔐
Password Power
Strong passwords are like secret codes! Use long passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols. Never share your passwords, even with friends!
🦠
Virus Defender
Computer viruses spread like real viruses! They can copy themselves and damage files. Keep your antivirus software updated like getting your shots!
👨‍💻
Hacking Alert
Hackers try to sneak into computers without permission! Use strong passwords and never share personal information online with strangers!
📶
WiFi Warrior
Public WiFi can be dangerous! Bad guys can see what you do on unsecured networks. Only use trusted WiFi and ask grown-ups first!
