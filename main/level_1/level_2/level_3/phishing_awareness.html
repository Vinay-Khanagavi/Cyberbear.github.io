Password Strength Awareness

Protect your accounts with strong passwords.

Ensure your passwords are secure by following these guidelines:

  • Use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays or pet names.
  • Make passwords at least 12 characters long.
  • Do not reuse passwords across multiple accounts.
  • Consider using a password manager to generate and store complex passwords.
  • Change passwords regularly, especially after a security breach.
  • Enable multi-factor authentication for added security.
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts that may try to steal your password.
